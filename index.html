♟ LT Item Mail ♟

Easily send items to distant players.

❯ Easily send items to distant players.

With LT Item Mail, you can mail items to another players.

You will need Vault installed + an Economy plugin to play with LT Item Mail v0.1.1+ (or newer versions).
And v0.2.0 (or newer versions) offers a language support system already.

❯ Looking for downloads?

Download the latest version Here or Here.

This plugin works on Bukkit & Spigot!
